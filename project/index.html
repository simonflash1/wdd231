About Us

We are a group of friends and students from Tucuman, Argentina. We love "Sanguches de Milanesa" a local way to say "Milanesa Sandwich" and went to a lot of restaurants and tried a lot of different Sanguches so we want to share our knowledge with others. We believe that knowledge is power and that sharing it is the key to success. We are dedicated to providing the best and up-to-date information about where to find the best restaurant for you.
